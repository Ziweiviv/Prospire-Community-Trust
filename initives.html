Our Initiatives

Prospire Community Trust is built around a set of interconnected initiatives that work together to strengthen community wellbeing, connection, education, and leadership development.

Each initiative plays a distinct role, but together they form a unified ecosystem that supports individuals, families, and the wider community to grow and thrive.

Playful Packs

A vibrant children’s radio show initiative that connects communities through radio, storytelling, and engaging activities, supporting learning, creativity

Explore Playful Packs

Family Network

A support and connection initiative designed to strengthen families by linking them with community resources, social opportunities, and practical support systems that reduce isolation and build resilience.

Explore Family Network

Community Champions Network

A community education and empowerment initiative that develops trusted local leaders. Community Champions provides practical learning in scam awareness, digital safety, and wellbeing, building confident individuals who can support others in their communities.

Explore Community Champions

Leadership Pathways Programme

A hands-on leadership development programme for emerging leaders. Participants gain experience in event coordination, project planning, teamwork, and community engagement, building practical leadership skills through active community involvement.

Explore Leadership Pathways
