Our Initiatives

Prospire Community Trust is built around a set of interconnected initiatives that work together to strengthen community wellbeing, connection, education, and leadership development.

Each initiative plays a distinct role, but together they form a unified ecosystem that supports individuals, families, and the wider community to grow and thrive.

Playful Packs

A vibrant children’s radio show initiative that connects communities through radio, storytelling, and engaging activities, supporting learning and creativity.

Explore Playful Packs

Family Network

A support and connection initiative designed to strengthen families by linking them with community resources, social opportunities, and practical support systems.

Explore Family Network

Community Champions Network

A series of workshops and educational sessions including digital safety, phishing prevention, wellbeing, and community mental health first responder training.

Explore Community Champions

Leadership Pathways Programme

A hands-on leadership development programme with mentoring, project planning, reflective practice, and real-world community project delivery.

Explore Leadership Pathways
