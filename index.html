Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower individuals and strengthen communities by providing accessible, high-quality programmes in technology, wellbeing, fitness, and community engagement. We aim to foster personal growth and a connected, vibrant community.

Our Vision

To build a vibrant community where individuals can grow, connect, and thrive through engaging programmes. We envision inspiring learning, promoting wellbeing, and fostering a sense of belonging for all.

Our Plan

Short-Term (1 Year)

Establish foundational services, secure initial funding, and build organisational infrastructure. Our goal is to launch our workshops and community initiatives, and start gathering feedback.

Long-Term (2+ Years)

Expand our offerings, enhance our community impact, and ensure financial sustainability. This includes increasing participation, improving programme quality, and growing community-driven initiatives.

Current State

Current Focus: Prospire Community Trust is focused on establishing foundational services, securing funding, and building our organisational structure. We are currently recruiting volunteers, creating partnerships, and conducting community outreach to increase engagement.
