Prospire Community Trust

A Christchurch-based charity bringing people together through events, workshops, and initiatives that build a thriving, connected community.

“He aha te mea nui o te ao. He tāngata, he tāngata, he tāngata”

What is the most important thing in the world? It is people, it is people, it is people.

Our Mission

To build connected and thriving communities through programmes that foster whanaungatanga, inspire participation, and create opportunities for people to prosper together.

Our Values

Prosperity

Supporting communities to grow, thrive, and achieve long-term wellbeing together.

Inspiration

Encouraging people to believe in their potential and lead positive change.

Whanaungatanga

Building strong relationships, connection, and belonging within communities.

Legal Entity & Governance

Prospire Community Trust is a registered New Zealand charitable trust governed by a Board of Trustees. The Board holds strategic oversight responsibility and ensures the organisation operates in line with its charitable purpose and legal obligations.

Day-to-day delivery is managed through a contracted operational role responsible for programme coordination, delivery, and community engagement across initiatives.

The Trust also works alongside volunteers and contractors who support the delivery of community programmes, events, and initiatives.
