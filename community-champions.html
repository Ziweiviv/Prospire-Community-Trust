Our Initiatives

Prospire Community Trust is built around a set of interconnected initiatives that work together to strengthen community wellbeing, connection, education, and leadership development.

Each initiative plays a distinct role, but together they form a unified ecosystem that supports individuals, families, and the wider community to grow and thrive.

Community Champions Network

Community Champions Network is a series of workshops and educational sessions designed to upskill community leaders and active residents so they can better support families and individuals in their local communities.

About the Programme

Community Champions Network delivers a series of practical workshops and educational sessions for community leaders, volunteers, and active residents.

Sessions include topics such as digital safety, phishing and scam prevention, community wellbeing, and mental health first responder awareness, alongside other locally relevant skills.

The purpose is to build practical capability and confidence so participants can better support others, respond to common community challenges, and act as trusted points of support within their local networks.

By strengthening local knowledge and leadership skills, the programme contributes to safer, more informed, and more connected communities.
